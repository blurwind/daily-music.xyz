PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: March 2024.
Onze Development ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Daily Music Discord Service (the "Service").
This document outlines the policies governing the collection, usage, and disclosure of personal data associated with the use of our Service, as well as the options available to users regarding their data.

1. Information Collection and Usage

We collect the following data to ensure the smooth operation of the Service and its systems:

If you have configured your server

The following data is collected only if you have configured your server:

  • Server ID: Identifies the server on which the Service is being used.
  • Channel ID: Determines the designated channel for sending music and trivia.
  • Role ID: Designates the role to ping for music and trivia.
  • Language: Determines which language the bot should use.
  • Genre: Defines the genre for the music.
  • Display: Specifies the display format for the music.
  • Display Mode: Specifies the display mode for the music (only if the display is set to "Card").
  • Status of the "Threads" option: Determines the status of the "Threads" option for music and trivia.
  • Status of the "Trivia" option: Determines the status of the "Trivia" option for trivia.
  • Mode of the "Trivia" option: Determines the mode of the "Trivia" option for trivia.
  • Status of the "Events" option: Determines the status of the "Events" option for events.
  • Number of music sent: Tracks the count of music sent for statistics.
  • Number of trivia sent: Tracks the count of trivia sent for statistics.
  • Number of events sent: Tracks the count of events sent for statistics.
  • Date of the last music sent: Records the date of the last music sent for statistics.
  • Date of the last trivia sent: Records the date of the last trivia sent for statistics.
  • Date of the last event sent: Records the date of the last events sent for statistics.
  • Encrypted webhook URL: To send the music, trivia, and events, this data is encrypted.

If you have played our game

The following data is collected only if you have played our game (Trivia of the Day):

  • User ID: Identifies the player.
  • Clicked: Indicates if the play button was clicked.
  • Guessed: Indicates if the music title was guessed.

2. Log Data & Deletion

Daily Music stores the aforementioned data only in specific cases (refer to the titles above).
It's important to note that once the Service leaves your server or you delete the configured channel, ALL data linked to that specific server will be PERMANENTLY deleted.
ALL data linked to users who played our game is deleted EVERY 24 hours.

3. Use of Data

The data we collect is solely used to ensure the proper functioning of the Service and to guarantee the smooth operation of its systems.

4. Data Disclosure

We do not disclose any collected data to third parties.
All data collected is used solely for the purpose of operating the Service and providing its services.

5. Security Measures

The data we store is not publicly available and is protected with appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Links to External Sites

Our Service may contain links to external sites not operated by us.
Clicking on a third-party link will direct you to that third party's site.
We strongly advise reviewing the privacy policy of any site you visit.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically.
Any changes will be published here.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact us through the following channels:

  • By joining the official Discord server.

  • By sending an e-mail to our mailbox.

  • By sending a message to our Twitter account.
