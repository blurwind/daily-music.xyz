PRIVACY POLICY

Effective date: February 2024.
Onze Development ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Daily Music Discord bot (the "Service").
This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect the following data to ensure the smooth operation of the bot and its system:

  • Server ID: This is used to identify the specific server on which the bot is being used.
  • ID of the channel for the music of the day: This is used to determine the designated channel to send the music of the day & the trivia of the day.
  • ID of the role for the music of the day: This is used to determine the designated role to ping for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day.
  • The number of musics of the day sent: For statistics.
  • The date of the last music of the day sent: For statistics.
  • The status of the "Threads" option: This is used to determine the status of the "Threads" option for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day.
  • ID of the thread created by the bot for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day (if enabled): This is used to determine the designated thread to send the music of the day & the trivia of the day.
  • The status of the "Trivia" option: This is used to determine the status of the "Trivia" option for the trivia of the day.
  • The genre defined for the music of the day: To adapt the music of the day to the defined genre.
  • The type of display selected for the music of the day.

2. Log Data

Daily Music stores the aforementioned data (Server ID, ID of the channel for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day, ID of the role for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day, the number of musics of the day sent, the date of the last music of the day sent, the status of the "Threads" option, the ID of the thread created by the bot for the music of the day & for the trivia of the day, the status of the "Trivia" option, the genre defined for the music of the day, and the type of display selected for the music of the day) only if the server has been configured to use the bot.
If the server configuration is reinitialized or if the bot leaves the server, ALL data linked to that specific server will be PERMANENTLY deleted.

3. Use of Data

The data we collect is solely used to ensure the proper functioning of the bot and to deliver the music of the day & the trivia of the day to the designated channel on the configured server.
This is to guarantee the smooth operation of the bot and its system.

4. Disclosure of Data

We do not disclose any data collected by the Service to third parties.
All collected data is used solely for the purpose of operating the bot and providing its services.

5. Security

The data we store is not publicly available and is protected with appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access.

6. Links to Other Sites

Our Service may contain links to other sites that are not operated by us.
If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site.
We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.
We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, here are the ways to contact us:

  • By joining the official Discord server.

  • By sending an e-mail to our mailbox.

  • By sending a message to our Twitter account.
