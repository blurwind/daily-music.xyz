Privacy Policy.

Effective date: May 2024.
Onze Development, referred to as "us", "we", or "our", manages Daily Music (referred to as the "Service").
This document delineates the policies governing the acquisition, utilization, and divulgence of personal data linked to the utilization of our Service,
alongside the available choices for users concerning their data

1. Information Collection and Usage

We collect the following data to ensure the smooth operation of the Service and its systems:

If you have configured your server:

    Server ID: Identifies the server on which the Service is being used.
    Channel ID: Determines the designated channel for sending music, trivia and events.
    Role ID: Designates the role to ping for music, trivia and events.
    Which daily messages should you be notified for: Specifies the types of daily messages that your server should be notified for.
    Language: Determines which language the application should use.
    Genre: Defines the genre for the music.
    Display: Specifies the display format for the music.
    Card Mode: Specifies the card mode for the music (only if the display is set to "Card").
    Card Background Image: Specifies the card background for the music (only if the display is set to "Card" and if the user configured it).
    Status of the "Threads" option: Determines the status of the "Threads" option for music, trivia and events.
    Status of the "Trivia" option: Determines the status of the "Trivia" option for trivia.
    Status of the "Poll" option: Determines the status of the "Poll" option for poll.
    Mode of the "Thread" option: Determines the mode of the "Thread" option for threads
    Mode of the "Trivia" option: Determines the mode of the "Trivia" option for trivia.
    Status of the "Events" option: Determines the status of the "Events" option for events.
    Number of music sent: Tracks the count of music sent for statistics.
    Number of trivia sent: Tracks the count of trivia sent for statistics.
    Number of events sent: Tracks the count of events sent for statistics.
    Number of polls sent: Tracks the count of poll sent for statistics.
    Date of the last music sent: Records the date of the last music sent for statistics.
    Date of the last trivia sent: Records the date of the last trivia sent for statistics.
    Date of the last event sent: Records the date of the last events sent for statistics.
    Date of the last poll sent: Records the date of the last poll sent for statistics.
    Encrypted webhook URL: To send the music, trivia, and events, this data is encrypted.

If you have played the TOTD game:

    User ID: Identifies the player.
    Clicked: Indicates if the "Guess" button was clicked.
    Guessed: Indicates if the music was guessed.

2. Data Logging and Deletion

We exclusively stores data as outlined in specific circumstances (see relevant headings above).
It is imperative to acknowledge that once the Service is removed from your server or the configured channel is deleted,
ALL data associated with that particular server will be PERMANENTLY eradicated.
Additionally, ALL data pertaining to users who engage with our game is automatically purged EVERY 24 hours.

3. Utilization of Data

The data we gather is strictly utilized to ensure the seamless functionality of the Service and to uphold the efficient operation of its systems.

4. Data Confidentiality

We uphold a strict policy of non-disclosure concerning any collected data to third parties.
All collected data is exclusively leveraged for the purpose of administering the Service and delivering its functionalities.

5. Security Protocols

The data stored by us is not publicly accessible and is safeguarded by robust security measures to mitigate unauthorized access.

6. External Site Links

Our Service may feature links to external websites not under our jurisdiction.
Selecting a third-party link will redirect users to the respective third-party site.
We strongly encourage a review of the privacy policy of any visited site.

7. Updates to the Privacy Policy

Periodically, we may revise our Privacy Policy. Any alterations will be promptly communicated through the official Discord server.
